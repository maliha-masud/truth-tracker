Truth Tracker is an ai based detection system aimed at analyzing Pakistani news content using machine learning models and sentiment analysis.
Fake news and misinformation are highly disruptive, and problems caused by fake news are on the rise due to the ever-increasing popularity of social media sites.
Therefore, the primary objective of this project is to detect fake news within textual content, identify bias and misinformation, and offer valuable insights into the underlying factors influencing the results obtained by the detector. This aims to enhance users' understanding and help combat misinformation.
