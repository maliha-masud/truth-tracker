What is Truth Tracker?

Truth Tracker is an AI-based detection system designed to identify misinformation, and fake news across various online platforms.

How does Truth Tracker work?

Truth Tracker uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze textual content. It evaluates the credibility of sources, fact-checks claims, and identifies patterns associated with misinformation.

What types of misinformation can Truth Tracker detect?

Truth Tracker can detect various forms of misinformation, including false claims, misleading information, and satire.

Is Truth Tracker accurate?

While Truth Tracker strives for accuracy, it's important to note that it is not perfect. Our AI algorithms continuously learn and adapt to new information to improve accuracy over time.

Does Truth Tracker only work for English content?

Currently, Truth Tracker primarily supports English-language content. However, we are continuously working to expand language support in future updates.

Is Truth Tracker free to use?

Yes, Truth Tracker is free to use for all users. We believe in promoting information transparency and combating misinformation for the benefit of society.
